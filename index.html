Program Details

  • Special Registration Fees:
    Some courses carry special fees in addition to the regular registration fees.
  • Payment by Credit Card:
    Acceptable forms of payment are American Express, Discover, Master Card, Visa, and eCheck. The 2% service fee is non-refundable. Credit cards are accepted for purchases in the campus bookstore ($15.00 or more). Charge card payments must be made in person or on the Web. They cannot be accepted by mail or phone.
  • Payment Plan
    The Nelnet Payment Plan is available to students who need an option for paying course and tuition fees/credit unit fees not covered by Financial Aid and/or other sources. There is a $40 per semester sign-up fee for using the Nelnet Payment Plan. For more information please visit MDC's Nelnet Payment page
  • Pay Via Western Union
    A great choice for International or out of state payments. Make payments directly to MDC via Western Union using the currency of your choice.
  • $30 Admission Application Processing Fee:
    All new college credit students are assessed a $30 non-refundable admission application processing fee. This fee must be paid when you submit the application.
  • $25 Bachelor's Degree In-Program Admission Application Processing Fee:
    All students admitted to an in-program Bachelor's degree are assessed a $25 non-refundable admission application processing fee.
  • $50 International Student Admission Application Processing Fee:
    All new international students are assessed a $50 non-refundable admission application processing fee.
  • $15 Per Credit - Virtual College Fee:
    Virtual College classes have a distance learning fee of $15 per credit. (i.e. $45 for a 3-credit course). To comply with federal requirements, the Virtual College uses secure login and password to verify the identity of online students. There are no additional student charges associated with verification of student identification.
  • $50 Late Registration Fee:
    College credit students registering on or after the first day of classes will be assessed a $50 non-refundable late registration fee. Please check the Academic Calendar for dates.
  • Resident Students / Non-Resident Students:
    Review information about Florida Residency for Tuition Purposes online.

All fees are subject to change without notice
